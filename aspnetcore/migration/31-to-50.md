## <a name="update-the-target-framework"></a><span data-ttu-id="8a983-114">대상 프레임 워크 업데이트</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8a983-114">Update the target framework</span></span>

```diff
-    <TargetFramework>netcoreapp3.1</TargetFramework>
+    <TargetFramework>net5.0</TargetFramework>
  </PropertyGroup>

```
